Present

Born and raised in Littleton, Colorado. I am a Colorado native and I take full advantage of everything this wonderful state has to offer.

I remember being introduced to HTML back in grade school and having a difficult time learning it. Never would I have thought back then that I would doing it as a career. I am very excited to have the opportunity to be part of this program.

I am an active snowboarder and sometimes you will see me ski. I love to camp, back-pack, back-country ski, raft, snowmobile, and dive. I jumped out of an airplane for the first time last year, and I am looking to become certified as soon as I start making money again.

I also love to travel, as it opens up the ability explore and learn different cultures.

I graduated from the University of Colorado, Boulder in 2011. B.A. Economics. While in school I worked at the Boulder Country Club and also sold snowboards at the second largest ski shop in Colorado. After graduation I went to work for Leanin' Tree Greeting Cards as a National Account Representative and recently resigned from my position to start this program.

Future

A career that gives me not just another job, but a lifetime of learning and innovating.

I want to use these skills to create a mentally stimulating career. A career that challenges me, because I can overcome these challenges.
